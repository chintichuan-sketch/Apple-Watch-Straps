Our Story

Founded in 2018, StrapCraft began when our founder couldn't find quality Apple Watch straps that combined style, comfort, and durability. We partner with master craftsmen who have decades of experience in watch strap manufacturing.

Every strap undergoes 12 quality checks and comes with our industry-leading 30-day comfort guarantee. We believe your watch strap should be as exceptional as the technology it accompanies.
